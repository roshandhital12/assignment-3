Skittles

Skittles are colorful, fruit-flavored candies that are a favorite among candy lovers worldwide.

Interesting Facts

  • Skittles were first produced in 1974 and were originally only available in the UK.
  • The slogan "Taste the Rainbow" was introduced in 1989 and is still used today.
  • Skittles come in a variety of flavors including strawberry, lemon, green apple, grape, and orange.
